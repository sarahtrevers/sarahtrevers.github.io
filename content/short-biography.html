A highly regarded and experienced Performer and Tutor

With an ABRSM Diploma in Music Performance, Sarah has performed since an early age at venues including the Royal Concert Hall, Albert Hall and Cathedral in Nottingham and the world-renowned Symphony Hall and Town Hall in Birmingham. She is available for Tuition in Singing, Piano and Violin from her home studio in the Vale of Belvoir, easily accessible from Nottingham, Newark on Trent, Grantham and Melton Mowbray.
