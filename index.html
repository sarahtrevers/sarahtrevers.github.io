Singing tuition

Classically trained in singing at Birmingham Conservatoire, Sarah offers vocal tuition in a variety of genres and at competitive rates. Tuition takes place either in her own home or at a location agreeable to both her and the pupil.

Piano tuition

Sarah is available for piano tuition, either at her home using her own upright piano, or at a venue agreeable to both her and the pupil. Tuition is available for those aged 6 and above.

Performance

As a soprano soloist, Sarah is available for performance in a wide range of events across a wide range of musical genres. Past events have included Weddings, Funerals and Corporate Events.
